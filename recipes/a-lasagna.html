Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried red lentils for the farts
  • 25 ounces jars marinara sauce
  • 1 cup raw cashews for the necessary cyanide
  • 14.5 ounces of firm tofy (use tempeh it's better, but it's not my recipe)
  • 1/2 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups of baby spinach
  • 1 box of lasagna sheets
  • Vegan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the red lentils
  2. Preheat the oven at 350 Fh
  3. Make the cashew-tofu ricotta

    Add the cashews to a food processor and process until fine and crumbly. Then add the tofu in chunks, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt, basil, oregano and garlic powder to the food processor. Pulse until well combined and pretty smooth. If it's too thick, add a few tablespoons of water to blend. Set aside.

  4. Assembling the lasagna

    Add about 1 cup of lentil marinara to the bottom of a large 9Ã—13 inch casserole dish. Spread it around evenly. Next add 4-5 lasagna noodles (uncooked). Spread half of the ricotta on top of the noodles. Top with half of the spinach. Add about 1 cup of the marinara sauce over the spinach, then place 4-5 lasagna noodles on top. Spread the rest of the ricotta over the noodles, then the rest of the spinach. Place 4-5 more noodles on top of the spinach, and then pour the rest of the sauce over the top, evenly.

  5. Cover tighly with foil and bake for 40 minutes
  6. When it's cooked cover the lasagna with cheese and place it back into the oven for 20 minutes
  7. Serve
